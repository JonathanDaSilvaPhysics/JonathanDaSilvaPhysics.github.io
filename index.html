- About me

I am currently working at Sogeti High Tech in Toulouse (AEROPARK), France as an engineer.

I held a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Laboratoire de Physique Subatomique et de Cosmologie (LPSC) in Grenoble, France. I was working more precisely in the Theoretical Physics group with Sabine Kraml.

Before this I held a postdoctoral position as a Marie Skłodowska-Curie early stage researcher within the MCnet network in the Particle Physics Group of the University of Manchester, United Kingdom, in the working group of Professor Michael Seymour.

I got my PhD from LAPTh, Annecy-le-Vieux, France, with Geneviève Bélanger as advisor.
