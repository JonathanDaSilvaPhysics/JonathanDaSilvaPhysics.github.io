- PhD thesis

I did my PhD thesis in theoretical physics at the Laboratoire d'Annecy-le-Vieux de Physique Théorique (LAPTh) between 2010 and 2013, under the supervision of Geneviève Bélanger.

I studied during these three years consequences of constraints coming from colliders, astroparticles and cosmology on some supersymmetric models and Dark Matter candidates.

I defended my PhD thesis at LAPTh on july 3, 2013. The thesis manuscript is available on TEL or on arXiv (link to the right).
